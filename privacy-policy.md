# Privacy Policy for Time Catchers

Last Updated: May 10, 2026

Time Catchers (“the App”) is a cozy collection game where users capture and collect visually satisfying times from screenshots taken on their device.

This Privacy Policy explains what information the App accesses, how it is used, and what rights users have regarding their data.

---

# 1. Information We Collect

Time Catchers is designed to be primarily local-first and privacy-friendly.

The App may access or process the following information:

## Screenshots and Images

With user permission, the App may access screenshots stored on the device in order to:

- Detect newly captured screenshots
- Read visible timestamps using on-device OCR (optical character recognition)
- Identify recognizable time patterns
- Generate collectible entries within the App

Screenshot analysis occurs locally on the user’s device whenever possible.

The App does not intentionally upload or transmit personal screenshots to remote servers.

---

# 2. OCR and Time Detection

The App uses on-device OCR technology to detect visible timestamps from screenshots.

Detected timestamps may be stored locally as part of the user’s collection history.

The App is designed specifically to detect time-related visual patterns and does not intentionally analyze unrelated personal content within screenshots.

---

# 3. Local Storage

Time Catchers stores gameplay-related information locally on the user’s device, including:

- Collected timestamps
- Collection history
- Theme preferences
- Settings
- Achievements or progression data
- Downloaded theme packs

This data remains on the device unless future optional cloud features are introduced.

---

# 4. In-App Purchases

Time Catchers may offer optional in-app purchases such as cosmetic theme packs.

Purchases are processed securely through:

- Google Play Billing
- Apple App Store / StoreKit

The App does not directly collect or store payment card information.

Purchase verification data may be temporarily processed to confirm ownership of purchased content.

---

# 5. Downloadable Theme Content

Optional purchased theme packs may be downloaded from remote content servers or CDNs.

Downloaded theme content may be cached locally on the device for offline use.

No personal screenshot data is transmitted as part of theme downloads.

---

# 6. Analytics and Diagnostics

The App may collect limited anonymous technical information for stability and debugging purposes, including:

- Crash reports
- Device type
- App version
- Performance diagnostics

This information does not intentionally identify individual users.

The App does not sell personal data.

---

# 7. Permissions

Depending on platform and operating system version, the App may request permissions such as:

- Photos / Media Library Access
- Notifications
- Storage Access

These permissions are used solely for core gameplay and App functionality.

Users may revoke permissions at any time through their device settings.

---

# 8. Children’s Privacy

Time Catchers is not directed toward children under the age required by local digital consent laws.

If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us so appropriate action can be taken.

---

# 9. Data Security

Reasonable efforts are made to protect locally stored data and purchased content.

However, no software or storage system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

---

# 10. Third-Party Services

The App may rely on third-party platform services including but not limited to:

- Google Play Services
- Google Play Billing
- Apple App Store
- Apple StoreKit
- ML Kit OCR

Use of these services may also be subject to their own privacy policies and terms.

Future versions of Time Catchers may introduce optional online features such as:

If these features are added, this Privacy Policy may be updated accordingly.
